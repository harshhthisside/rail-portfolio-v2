Why RailScape?

RailScape is my personal railway journal. The aim isn't simply to review trains—it's to document journeys as they happened, highlighting landscapes, locomotive changes, station operations, onboard experiences and the countless little moments that make railway travel memorable.

Whether it's a Rajdhani crossing the Konkan Railway during the monsoon or an overnight express through freight corridors, every trip tells a different story.
